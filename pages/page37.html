The Steps to selling : Pre Meet planning

The quick pre-meet planning gives the salesperson a clear and detailed picture of each customer, so that a customized story can be built for each one

This Benefits in :

  1. Reduction of uncertainty
  2. Higher quality of meeting
  3. Better interpretation of the product in terms of the end user requirement
  4. Increased confidence of the salesperson
  5. Sounder selling in shorter and more successful during the meeting
