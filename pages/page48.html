The Steps to selling: Gaining Commitment Steps

Step 1
  • Review the benefits accepted by the customer

    • When you attempt to gain commitment, it is crucial to review all the benefits accepted by the customer during the sales call.

    In your review, you do not need to include features.
Step 2
  • Ask for action

    • A request for action is actually a paraphrased sales call objective. When you ask for action you must be specific.
