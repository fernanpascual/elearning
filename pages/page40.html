The Steps to selling : Probing Function

The general purpose of probing is to uncover customer needs and concerns.

  1. It allows you to guide the customer to reveal his needs.
  2. With effective probing skills, you take control of the sales meeting.
Type Definition Example
OPEN Allows the customer to describe a need

Sales rep:
“Sir, what sort of problems do you have with your travel flight bookings?”
Buyer:
“High prices is the major complaint.”
CLOSE Allows you to direct the customer to a presumed need Sales rep:
“Do your customers ever complain about high cost?”
Buyer:
”Yes”
