Tips to Sell

  1. You are selling your product not the visual aid.
    2.
  2. Know your visual aid details
    3.
  3. Use it to support what you say, not to do the selling for you.
    4.
  4. Make it clean and ready to use.
    5.
  5. Use pen or marker, if required
    6.
  6. Keep control of the visual aid.
    7.
  7. Make it concise with what you are saying.
    8.
  8. Rap it unless it is in use.
    9.
  9. Make it seen by your audience not you.
    10.
  10. Use it to illustrate point by point.
    11.
