The Steps to selling : Probing

Probing is the skill of questioning

Types of Probes:

Type Definition Example
OPEN A question that invites an extended explanation

Sales Rep:
“Sir, what are the different products that you are dealing with?”
Buyer:
“there are several, we deal from range of selling automobile parts to travel agency.”
CLOSE A question that can be answered in a single word, often “yes” or “no” Sales Rep:
“Do you deal with booking bus tickets?”
Buyer:
yes
